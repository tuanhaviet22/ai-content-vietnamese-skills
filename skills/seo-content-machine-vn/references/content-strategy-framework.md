# Content Strategy Framework

Use this framework after SERP analysis and before writing.

## Persona Definition

Define the reader by:

- role
- business stage
- current workflow
- pain level
- budget sensitivity
- decision power
- knowledge level

Example:

```text
Persona: Chu shop online 1-10 nhan su, dang ban qua Facebook va san TMĐT, da co don deu nhung van quan ly bang Excel va tin nhan.
```

## Search Maturity

Classify the reader:

- Problem-aware: knows the pain, not the solution.
- Solution-aware: knows the category, comparing methods.
- Product-aware: comparing vendors or offers.
- Ready-to-buy: needs proof, pricing, and CTA.

## Goal Mapping

Traffic goal:

- Prioritize comprehensive answer and broad topical coverage.
- Use soft CTA and internal links.

Sales goal:

- Prioritize buying criteria, objections, proof, and next step.
- Include product naturally after problem and criteria are clear.

Branding goal:

- Prioritize point of view, authority, unique insight, and memorable frameworks.
- Use lighter CTA.

## Angle Selection

Good angles for Vietnamese SEO content:

- beginner-friendly and practical
- avoid wasting money
- small business first
- compare before buying
- step-by-step implementation
- checklist-driven
- local examples and local objections

Choose one primary angle. Do not mix too many angles in one article.

## Hook Formula

Use one of these:

- Pain hook: "Neu ban dang gap X, van de khong chi la Y ma la Z."
- Cost hook: "Moi ngay lam X thu cong co the lam ban mat Y."
- Mistake hook: "Nhieu nguoi chon X vi gia re, nhung bo qua Y."
- Practical hook: "Bai nay giup ban chon X theo 5 tieu chi de dung duoc ngay."

## CTA Strategy

Soft CTA:

- best for informational content
- examples: download checklist, read related guide, get consultation

Medium CTA:

- best for commercial investigation
- examples: try free, compare plan, request demo

Direct CTA:

- best for transactional content
- examples: start trial, book demo, contact sales

## Internal Link Suggestions

Suggest links by intent:

- problem article -> solution guide
- solution guide -> product page
- product page -> pricing or demo
- comparison article -> case study or buying guide
- beginner guide -> checklist or template
